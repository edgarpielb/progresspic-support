# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

ProgressPic ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Photos and Media
- **Progress Photos**: Photos you capture or import into the app are stored locally on your device and in your iCloud account (if iCloud sync is enabled)
- **Photo Metadata**: Date, time, and journey associations for your photos

### Health Data
- **HealthKit Data**: If you grant permission, we access health data including:
  - Weight
  - Body fat percentage
  - BMI (Body Mass Index)
  - Body measurements
- This data is read from and may be written to Apple Health, but remains under your control

### Usage Data
- App usage statistics and activity tracking (stored locally)
- Photo-taking streaks and journey progress

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the app's core functionality
- To sync your data across devices using iCloud (when enabled)
- To display your progress photos, measurements, and health metrics
- To generate activity statistics and streaks
- To improve app performance and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Your photos and data are stored locally on your device using SwiftData
- **iCloud Sync**: If enabled, your data is synced to your personal iCloud account using CloudKit. We do not have access to your iCloud data
- **No External Servers**: We do not transmit, store, or process your photos or personal data on external servers
- **HealthKit**: Health data remains in Apple Health and is only accessed with your explicit permission

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your personal information with third parties.** Your data remains:
- On your device
- In your personal iCloud account (if sync is enabled)
- In Apple Health (if you grant permission)

## Your Rights and Choices

- **Camera Access**: You can revoke camera permissions in iOS Settings
- **Photo Library Access**: You can revoke photo library permissions in iOS Settings
- **HealthKit Access**: You can revoke HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health
- **iCloud Sync**: You can disable iCloud sync in the app settings
- **Data Deletion**: You can delete journeys, photos, and measurements at any time within the app

## Children's Privacy

ProgressPic is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app or via email.
