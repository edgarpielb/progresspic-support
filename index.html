Frequently Asked Questions

Open the app and create a new Journey by tapping the + button. Give your journey a name, then use the camera to capture your first progress photo. The app will save your photo and you can start tracking your transformation!
Yes! ProgressPic uses iCloud to sync your data across all your Apple devices automatically. Make sure you're signed into the same iCloud account on all devices and have iCloud enabled in Settings. You can check your sync status in the app's Settings.
Within any Journey, you can add measurements like weight, body fat percentage, and body measurements (chest, waist, hips, arms, legs). Tap on a journey to view it, then add measurements. You can also connect HealthKit to import weight and body composition data automatically.
Absolutely! You can set custom reminders for each Journey. Choose the days and time you want to be reminded, and ProgressPic will send you a notification to take your progress photo.
Use the Compare feature within your Journey to see your transformation side by side. You can also use the Watch feature to see your photos play as a slideshow, or export a timelapse video of your progress.
Yes! ProgressPic can create timelapse videos of your progress photos. Go to your Journey, use the Watch or Compare features, and export your transformation as a video to share with friends or on social media.
ProgressPic stores all your data locally on your device and in your private iCloud account. We don't collect, track, or have access to your photos or personal information. Your data stays yours. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
You can delete individual photos, measurements, or entire Journeys directly within the app. To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device. Your iCloud data will sync the deletion across devices. For complete removal from iCloud, you can manage iCloud storage in your device Settings.
