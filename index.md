# ProgressPic

Welcome to ProgressPic - your personal fitness journey companion.

## About

ProgressPic is an iOS app designed to help you track your fitness and body transformation progress through photos, measurements, and health metrics. Capture your journey, compare your progress over time, and stay motivated with visual evidence of your hard work.

## Features

- **Photo Journeys**: Create multiple progress journeys and capture photos to track your transformation
- **HealthKit Integration**: Sync with Apple Health to track weight, body fat percentage, BMI, and other body composition metrics
- **Measurement Tracking**: Log body measurements and compare them over time
- **Visual Comparisons**: Compare photos side-by-side to see your progress
- **Activity Tracking**: Monitor your photo-taking streaks and activity
- **iCloud Sync**: Your data is securely synced across all your devices using iCloud
- **Privacy First**: Your photos and data stay on your device and in your iCloud account

## Getting Started

1. Download ProgressPic from the App Store
2. Grant camera permissions to capture progress photos
3. Optionally connect HealthKit to sync body metrics
4. Create your first journey and start capturing your progress!
